Steak

Description

A great flank steak marinade like this one is important if you want a tender, juicy, flavorful steak. Make sure you marinate your flank steak for at least 2 hours for best results or longer if you have time. This wonderful quick and easy recipe also works great when the steak is sliced and used for fajitas.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Lemon juice
  • Worcestshire sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • Garlic
  • Black pepper
  • Skirt steak

Steps

  1. Whisk together all seasonings and sauces.
  2. Pour marinade over flank steak in a baking dish. Let it sit for 2 to 6 hours.
  3. Preheat a grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  4. Cook steak for 5 minutes on each side.
  5. Slice and serve.
